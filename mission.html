Purpose & Vision

Calling the Church.
Reaching the World.

"The broad sweep of my writing is to understand contemporary culture — both secular and religious — and how they interact and influence each other. My mission is to call the Evangelical Church to understand the secular world in order to reach it, not join it in its folly."
01

Light & Salt

Dr. Thompson wants the Church to do its job: to be the light of the world and the salt of the earth, and not a cheerleader for wickedness in high places.

02

Truth to Power

Dr. Thompson is a person of faith who takes the Bible to be true. He believes that if he is to speak truth to power, it must be God's truth — the truth that changes the world, not his own. That is his goal: to bring God's truth to the people. He points to the example of Christ, who spoke truth to power but made it always about the One who says, "I am the way, the life, and the Truth."

The Wisdom of Humility

How Faith Shapes the Work

God's Truth to the People

Dr. Thompson is a person of faith who takes the Bible to be true. He believes that if he is to speak truth to power, it must be God's truth — the truth that changes the world, not his own. That is his goal: to bring God's truth to the people.

He points to the example of Christ, who spoke truth to power but made it always about the One who says, "I am the way, the life, and the Truth." Dr. Thompson wants readers to take a long look at Christ in the wisdom of His love, grace, mercy, and forgiveness, and to extend that same spirit to others.

"I am all things to all people, that I might by all means save some." — Paul the Apostle

Reaching the Audience

Any Platform, Every Opportunity

Dr. Thompson plans to reach his audience any way he can — guided by Paul's words: "I am all things to all people that I might by all means save some." He is available and eager for:

Book Tours

Meeting readers and speaking about the issues driving his work across the country.

Talk Shows

Engaging media on faith, culture, wisdom, and the Evangelical Church's role in society.

Church Engagements

Preaching, teaching, and leading conversations with congregations on his books' themes.

Lectures

Speaking on specific topics of interest — theology, communication, culture, AI and human identity.

Media Interviews

Radio, television, and podcast conversations on faith, loss, purpose, and the state of wisdom today.

✦

Books Behind Bars

Dr. Thompson has a special initiative called the Books Behind Bars (BBB) Program, through which he donates copies of his books to prisoners and seeks opportunities to engage them in conversation on the topics of the books.

This initiative flows from his decades of prison outreach ministry — including at the famed Joliet Maximum Security Prison — and his deep belief that truth and wisdom belong to every person, regardless of circumstance.
